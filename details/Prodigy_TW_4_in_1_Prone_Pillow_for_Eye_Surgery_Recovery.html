Prodigy TW 4-in-1 Prone Pillow

Your trusted partner for recovery and comfort after eye surgeries.

Designed for Post-Operative Comfort

This Prone Pillow by Prodigy TW is specially made to support patients during recovery from eye surgeries such as vitrectomy or retinal detachment. Its contoured middle section ensures the eye mask doesn't press directly against the eyes, providing a safe and secure fit for better rest.

The unique shape of the pillow reduces pressure on the neck and shoulders, helping you sleep more comfortably and avoid unnecessary strain. Whether at home or on the go, this pillow is a versatile solution for those who need post-operative support in multiple positions.

4-in-1 Functionality for All-Day Support

With its innovative 4-in-1 configuration, this prone pillow can be used in back, side, stomach, or face-down positions, making it ideal for a variety of recovery needs. The elongated design is perfect for face-down sleeping, which is essential for eye surgery patients.

Its lightweight and portable build allows easy transport, and the included storage bag ensures you can take it to the hospital, bed, or even on travel trips. The breathable fabric and hidden zipper make it simple to clean, keeping it hygienic for long-term use.

Key Features

  • Ergonomically designed for comfortable face-down sleeping
  • Adjustable 4-in-1 configuration for back, side, and stomach positions
  • Lightweight and portable with included storage bag
  • Breathable fabric with hidden zipper for easy cleaning
  • EU certified for safety and hygiene
