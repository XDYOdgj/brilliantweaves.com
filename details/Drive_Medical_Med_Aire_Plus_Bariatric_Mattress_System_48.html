Drive Medical Med Aire Plus Bariatric Mattress System

The Med Aire Plus Bariatric Mattress System is engineered to deliver exceptional pressure relief and support for patients with complex medical needs. With a weight capacity of up to 750 lbs, it's built to handle the demands of bariatric care while ensuring comfort and safety in both clinical and home environments.

Its advanced cell-on-cell design promotes optimal pressure redistribution, helping to prevent and manage pressure ulcers, even in advanced stages. The mattress includes a digital pump with diagnostic alarms, offering personalized care and automatic adjustment to alternating pressure mode if left static for more than 2 hours. This feature enhances patient well-being by maintaining consistent support and reducing the risk of skin breakdown.

Featuring 10\" deep air cells, the Med Aire Plus system prevents bottoming out, allowing for extended use without compromising comfort. The pillow function ensures that three air cells remain inflated at the head of the bed during static mode, providing extra support for the upper body. It is compatible with most hospital beds and offers easy customization to suit individual patient requirements.

Key Benefits of the Med Aire Plus Bariatric Mattress System:

  • Alternating pressure technology for effective pressure ulcer prevention and relief
  • 10\" deep air cells prevent bottoming out and offer long-term support
  • Digital pump with diagnostic alarms ensures customizable care
  • Pillow function maintains comfort by keeping three air cells inflated at the head
  • Heavy-duty construction designed for extended use and reliability

Whether in a hospital, long-term care facility, or at home, the Med Aire Plus system is a trusted choice for improving patient outcomes and comfort. As a low air loss mattress, it balances pressure relief with controlled airflow, supporting skin health and promoting overall recovery.
